Gamejam Game

This was my first adventure game.
We took a BulletHell as a reference for this game.
So in this game you have too dodge all the bullets that are incoming and shoot the slimes dead to get to the boss and win the level.
We made a protoype of 1 level that works.

When you start you get 1 weapon to shoot at the enemies there is unlimited ammo but you have to reload everytime the mag is empty
you have also a dodge to use you can pick up weapons that are dropped from enemies.

Defeat all the slimes on the map to get to the boosfight.
there are different sizes so there is a differents in HP
The slimes shoot slime bullets and there is a lot if you encounter them all in once so the key is to deal with 1 slime at a time.

If you at the bossfight the background music changes to boss music.
the fairy boss has 3 stages: normal shooting bullets and drop explosion bullets, agressive stage spawn mini enemies and they can shoot bullets too and wave attacks in a circle, defensive stage spawn mini enemies more and passive attack and stay in distance.
if you defeat the boss you win the game

The pixel art assets where built by me and I made 10 assets myself. I build them with paint.net and got other assets from assets.io for free.

Coding is done by the other 3 men we got in total 6 scripts.
We have got also a gamemanager that manages the slimes, boss, points stats of the player, bossfight activation and the end results.
There is a script with spawn management where I can change the spawns of the slimes and the boss mini enemies .
